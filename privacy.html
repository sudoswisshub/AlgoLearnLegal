Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

At AlgoLearn, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy details how we handle information in our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in absolute data privacy. AlgoLearn is a localized study application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal identification information or analytics data to external servers. No user accounts are required to use the app.

2. Local Application Data

All learning progress, spaced repetition intervals, reviews, custom notes, stats, and settings preferences are stored locally on your device (using standard iOS storage technologies like Core Data or UserDefaults). This information never leaves your device unless you choose to back up your device through iCloud or iTunes backups.

3. Third-Party Services

The application does not integrate with any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. Your study behaviors, session lengths, and flashcard performance remain entirely private to you.

4. Notifications

If you choose to enable Review Reminders, the app schedules local notifications directly on your device. These notifications do not use remote push servers, meaning no notification data is transmitted over the internet.

5. Security of Your Data

Since all data is saved locally on your iOS device, the security of your data relies on the security protocols built into the iOS operating system. We recommend keeping your iOS device updated and using passcode/Face ID features to protect your local data.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy inside the app. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
